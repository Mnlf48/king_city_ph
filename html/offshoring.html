Why Offshore with Kings City

The Smarter Way to Scale

The Philippines offers an exceptional talent pool of English-speaking, highly educated professionals who are eager to work with international companies. With Kings City, you get the infrastructure, HR support, and facilities management — so you can focus on your business.

Our clients are primarily from Australia, but we help businesses from all around the world build high-performing, dedicated teams that feel like a natural extension of their local team.

How It Works

Your Journey to Offshoring

From initial consultation to a fully operational team — in as little as 6 weeks.

Discovery & Scoping
