Kings City Story
Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to a Nationwide Community

Kings Group of Companies was founded in 1999 with a simple yet powerful mission — to provide jobs and create meaningful opportunities for Filipinos.

In 2009, we expanded that vision to include Kings City, a premium co-working and offshoring destination in Parañaque City. Today, we support more than 10,000 employees nationwide and help businesses from Australia and around the world build high-performing teams in the Philippines.

We are not just a co-working space. We are a community of workforce and workspace — a place where connections are made, businesses are built, and careers are launched.

Our Journey

Milestones & Growth

1999

Kings Group Founded

Kings Group of Companies is established in the Philippines, beginning its journey of providing quality employment opportunities.

2009

The Kings Community Launches

The Kings expands beyond recruitment to form a growing community of jobs across the country.

2018

Kings City Opens

Kings City — our premium co-working and offshoring space in Parañaque City — opens its doors to the public.

2020

Offshoring Division Grows

We launch our dedicated offshoring solution, helping Australian and international companies build teams in the Philippines.

Today

10,000+ Employees Nationwide

Kings Group now supports over 10,000 employees across the Philippines, with virtual offices, co-working, events and offshoring services.
