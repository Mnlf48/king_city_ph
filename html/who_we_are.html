Who We Are

More Than a Workspace — A Community Built for Growth

Founded in 1999, Kings Group of Companies began with a simple yet powerful mission — to provide jobs and create meaningful opportunities for Filipinos. What started as a microfinancing cooperative in Zamboanga City has grown into a nationwide network of businesses.

In 2020, we launched Kings City — a premium co-working and offshoring destination in Parañaque City. Today, we support more than 10,000 members nationwide and help businesses from Australia and around the world build high-performing teams in the Philippines.

  • 10,000+ members across the Philippines
  • Premium coworking, events & office spaces
  • Offshoring solutions for global businesses
  • Worker-owned cooperative model
